About Us

PR Law Associates is a multi-faceted law firm with office in Chennai. Advocate V.Ponraj & PR Law Associates is committed to providing its client with effective, competent and professional service through a combination of personal attention, detail-oriented service and accountability. Clients should feel confident that their legal matters will be handled with utmost attention and skill. Advocate V.Ponraj and PR Law Associates strives to provide Quality legal service in an expeditious manner. Advocate V.Ponraj and PR Law Associates values the professional relationship it maintains with its clients. We understand that the lawyer-client relationship is based on trust, respect and confidentiality. This lawyer-client relationship requires immediate attention to client concerns which is why advocates/attorneys from Advocate V.Ponraj and PR Law Associates make it a to promptly return all telephone calls and emails, as well as updating clients on the progress of their matters.

Established in 2015 Advocate V.Ponraj and PR Law Associates have established themselves within a short span of time in the field of consultancy services which includes Civil, Criminal , personal, property and matrimonial laws , banking and recovery disputes along with Constitutional law litigation and advising in India. Advocate V.Ponraj and PR Law Associates started with the goal of delivering exceptional client. Service through innovative and customized legal solution. Also our firm believes in putting up a case strongly with a strategy and As a result, we believe that we are strongly positioned to understand and address the concerns and issue facing entities operating in Indians rapidly changing business and legal environment.
